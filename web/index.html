Vision

To realise an equal-opportunity platform that lends a helping hand to
people from all sections of the society, without bias or prejudice, to
identify and pursue their passions, thereby adding value to their lives
and the society that they are part of.

Mission

  1. Create an online platform that is responsive and device
    independent, that enables an individual to organise and participate
    in events that help other individuals learn valuable skills.

  2. Facilitate the exchange of ideas, skills, education, products
    and services that enrich the participant's lives and benefits their
    communities.
